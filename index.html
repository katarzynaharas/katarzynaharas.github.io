Governing the Workaround
Requirements Engineering Framework for ERP/TMS Operational Gaps

Explored the development of workarounds around ERP and TMS systems in operational environments — why employees create unofficial processes outside formal systems, how these workarounds evolve over time, and how they affect operational transparency and efficiency.

Designed a requirements engineering framework to categorise operational workarounds, enabling structured governance, identification of process gaps, and clear prioritisation for leadership decision-making. The framework created visibility into pain points previously impossible to measure or communicate across hierarchies.

🎯 Structured leadership visibility into unofficial processes · Systemic gaps prioritised and surfaced · Framework bridges operations, systems, and human behaviour
